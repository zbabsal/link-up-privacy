This Privacy Policy explains how LinkUp collects, uses, shares, and protects personal data through the LinkUp mobile application and related services.

1. Introduction

LinkUp places great importance on the privacy of our users' personal data. This policy describes the personal data we collect, use, share, and protect through the LinkUp mobile application and related services. By using our App, you agree to the data collection and use terms outlined in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects different types of personal data to provide, improve, and secure our services.

Data Provided by the User:

  • Your name, email address, username, password, profile photo, and biography.
  • All shares ("Links"), reactions, private messages, and comments you create within the App.
  • Customer support requests or other communications established with the App.

Automatically Collected Data:

  • Precise Geographical Location (GPS): Collected only with your explicit consent, due to the core functionality of our App (displaying nearby Links and creating location-based Links). You reserve the right not to share your location, but some essential features may be limited.
  • App usage statistics (duration of time spent, Links clicked, profiles viewed, search queries, and interaction statistics).
  • Your IP address, device model, operating system, language settings, mobile network information, and unique device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for:

  • Service Provision: Managing your LinkUp accounts and providing basic services.
  • Location-Based Matching: Displaying nearby Links.
  • Personalization: Offering personalized Link suggestions.
  • Security and Compliance: Preventing fraud, complying with laws.
  • Application Development Purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.
  • User Visibility: The Links you create, your profile information, and the Reactions you give to Links may be visible to other LinkUp users depending on your application settings. Your precise location is not directly displayed on your profile.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: Data may be shared with third-party service providers (hosting, analytics) we use to support the operation of the App.
  • Legal Necessity: We may also share your data upon legal necessity, court order, or request from a government agency.

5. Use of Location Data

Your location data is used to perform LinkUp's core feature: adding the correct geographical tag to your Links and bringing other nearby Links to your feed. Your location data may be kept in a generalized format after the service is provided and is not directly linked to your identity. You can withdraw your location sharing permission at any time through your device's settings.
